    Privacy Policy

    1. Data We Process

    We process the files you submit, standard technical metadata (including IP addresses and browser agents), customer support emails, and payment-related metadata provided by our payment gateway.

    2. How We Utilize Your Data

    We use this data strictly to format your cleaned output files, generate data previews, maintain the security of our platform, investigate support requests, and comply with legal requirements.

    3. Ephemeral Storage & Wipes

    Your uploaded files and processed data are stored on a temporary basis. All data is automatically purged from our servers after a limited retention window, generally 72 hours.

    4. Payment Security

    All transactions are securely handled by Stripe. We never store your full payment card details on flashfix.io servers.

    5. Data Sharing

    We will never sell your data. We only share information with trusted infrastructure providers strictly to operate and secure the service.

    6. Security Measures & Your Rights

    We deploy strict operational safeguards including HTTPS encryption, secure access controls, and automated file destruction protocols. Contact us anytime at [email protected].

    2. Service & Delivery

    Our platform allows you to upload supported CSV files, view a processed preview, and pay to unlock the finalized output. Pricing is dynamically displayed before checkout. Service delivery is considered legally complete the moment your processed file becomes available for download.

    Refund Policy

    1. General Guidelines

    Because our platform generates a transparent, 20-row data preview before you enter any payment information, refunds on digital deliveries are strictly limited.

    2. Eligible Refund Scenarios

    • · A technical failure on our infrastructure prevents delivery of your finalized file.
    • · A billing error caused you to be charged multiple times for the same job.
    • · The downloaded output is completely unusable due to a processing failure on our end.

    3. Ineligible Refund Scenarios

    • · You accidentally uploaded the incorrect file or an outdated version.
    • · The result was delivered accurately but your requirements subsequently changed.
    • · You successfully downloaded the file but decided you no longer need it.
    • · The file processed correctly but does not natively fit your internal workflows.

    4. Requesting a Review

    Email [email protected] with your file name, upload time, Job ID, a description of why the file failed, and any screenshots. We aim to review all requests within 1 to 3 business days.
