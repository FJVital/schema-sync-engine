    • · The exact name of your uploaded file
    • · The approximate time you attempted the upload
    • · Your specific Job ID, if the system provided one
    • · A brief explanation of the problem
    • · Any screenshots or error messages

    We typically respond within 1–3 business days. For security matters, include "Security" in the subject line.

    Privacy Policy

    1. Data We Process

    We process the files you submit, standard technical metadata, customer support emails, and payment-related metadata provided by our payment gateway.

    2. How We Utilize Your Data

    We use this data strictly to format your cleaned output files, generate data previews, maintain platform security, investigate support requests, and comply with legal requirements.

    3. Ephemeral Storage

    Your uploaded files and processed data are stored on a temporary basis and automatically purged after a retention window of generally 72 hours.

    4. Payment Security

    All transactions are handled by Stripe. We never store your full payment card details on flashfix.io servers.

    5. Data Sharing

    We will never sell your data. We only share information with trusted infrastructure providers strictly to operate the service.

    6. Security & Rights

    We deploy HTTPS encryption, secure access controls, and automated file destruction. Contact us at support@flashfix.io.

    Terms of Service

    1. Acceptable Use

    flashfix.io must only be used for legal and authorized purposes. You maintain full responsibility for the data you upload and may not reverse-engineer or misuse the application.

    2. Service & Delivery

    Our platform allows you to upload CSV files, view a processed preview, and pay to unlock the finalized output. Service delivery is complete the moment your processed file becomes available for download.

    3. Data Vaulting & Storage

    • · Guest Users: Data is ephemeral and purged within 24 hours.
    • · Registered Members: Files are securely vaulted in AWS cloud, accessible via Cloud Vault History.
    • · Zero AI Training Guarantee: Your data is never used to train or refine AI models.

    4. Refunds & Limitations

    Because flashfix.io provides a free, live data preview prior to payment, refunds are highly restricted. Our maximum liability will never exceed the specific amount paid for the job in question.

    Refund Policy

    1. General Guidelines

    Because our platform generates a transparent, 20-row data preview before payment, refunds on digital deliveries are strictly limited.

    2. Eligible Refund Scenarios

    • · A technical failure prevents delivery of your finalized file.
    • · A billing error caused multiple charges for the same job.
    • · The output is completely unusable due to a processing failure on our end.

    3. Ineligible Refund Scenarios

    • · You accidentally uploaded the incorrect file.
    • · The result was delivered accurately but your requirements changed.
    • · You downloaded the file but no longer need it.

    4. Requesting a Review

    Email support@flashfix.io with your file name, upload time, Job ID, and a description of the issue. We review all requests within 1–3 business days.
